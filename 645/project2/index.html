Internet Influencers

1976

Apple Founded

Steve Jobs founds Apple Inc. alongside Steve Wozniak with a vision to bring computers to the mass market through beautiful, accessible design.

1984

Macintosh Personal Computer

Apple released the first personal computer to include a mouse and window based graphical user interface.

1989

The World Wide Web Is Invented

Tim Berners-Lee begins development on the World Wide Web. Within 10 months the first website, a download page for the WWW prototype, is released.

1998

Google Is Formed

The search engine giant was founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin.

2007

Google Aquires DoubleClick

Advertising service DoubleClick was purchased by Google for $3.1 billion. With the companies vast personal data utilization, the internet data and advertising landscape would forever be changed.
